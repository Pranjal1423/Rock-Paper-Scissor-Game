## How to Play?

1. Choose your move by clicking on **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors**.
2. The computer will randomly pick its move.
3. The result of the match will be displayed:
   - Your move
   - Computer's move
   - The result (Win, Lose, or Tie)
